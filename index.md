# Privacy Policy for Endurien: DoD Tracker

**Last Updated:** December 28, 2025

## Introduction

Endurien: DoD Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

Endurien collects the following types of data:

### Workout Session Data

- Date and time of workouts
- Duration (minutes)
- Calories burned
- Activity type (Strength, Cardio, Yoga, Walk, Other)
- Optional notes you enter

### Apple Health Data (Optional)

- If you choose to sync with Apple Health, we read:
  - Workout data (type, duration, calories, date)
  - This data is imported with your explicit permission

### Challenge Configuration

- Challenge track selection (EPIC, 300, or 200)
- Challenge start date
- Progress tracking data

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite.**

- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No cloud sync or backup
- No data is stored on our servers
- Your data never leaves your device

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.**

- No data sharing with advertisers
- No data sharing with analytics services
- No data sharing with other apps
- All data remains on your device

## Apple Health Integration

If you choose to sync with Apple Health:

- We **read** workout data from Apple Health (with your permission)
- We **store** imported workouts locally in the app
- We **never write** data back to Apple Health
- You can revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

## Data Export

Data export functionality is coming soon in a future update. For now, all data remains stored locally on your device.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

1. Delete the app from your device

When you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

**Note:** Challenge reset functionality is coming soon, which will allow you to reset your challenge without deleting the app.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
